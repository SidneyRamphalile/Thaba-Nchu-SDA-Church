WHO ARE WE?


Seventh-day Adventists are a global family of Christians who hold the Bible as the ultimate authority. Adventists grow their relationship with Jesus by continually turning to the Bible, believing it's the key to understanding His character.


2015 TOC Pathfinder Fair

In 2015, the Trans Orange Conference of Seventh-Day Adventists (TOC) Pathfinder fair was held in Mpumalanga, one of the Provinces in South Africa. We competed against 135 clubs and the Thaba Nchu SDA church Pathfinders won position one and brought the trophy home. Mrs. Mapule Segalo was the Pathfinder Director and Ms. Tshwaranang Peme was the assistant Director. The club has 11 members, only two of the members were Seventh-Day Adventist kids and the rest were children from Ratlou community. The event took place from 16 to 19 July 2015. Our Adventurers did not win but they had a wonderful time.
