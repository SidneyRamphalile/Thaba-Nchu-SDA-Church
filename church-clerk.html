CHURCH CLERK

The Church Clerk, like other church officers, is a servant of God called to share in and support the activities of the church. God has created us for service. The clerk is to familiarise themselves with the method used for reporting and organising records in the local church. The church clerk works closely with the pastor, church officers and members. He/she reports to the church board and the local conference. Assistant clerks report to the church clerk. The church clerk is responsible for:

  • ensuring that the church's records are maintained and the church's business is documented.
  • working with lists, changes, numbers and people.
