THABA NCHU SDA CHURCH MISSIONS

Thaba Nchu SDA church has been envolved in evangelism, in events such as Road Shows, Food Parcel Distributions and Campaigns, ultimately resulting in Baptisms. Below is a gallery of mission events the church has been envolved in.

1. 2016 Rooifontein Roadshow.

In 2016, the church created a Road Show during the December holidays, where people from the community of Rooifontein - an area in Thaba Nchu town, gathered together in the evening, under the bright night sky twinkling with lovely stars, and watched Cosmic Conflict- a powerful presentation that takes you back in time, to Heaven, and explains how evil originated in God's perfect and beautiful universe and shows you how God has made a way for you to be saved and regain lost paradise when Jesus returns. The church beamed a projector on the wall of a certain house in the community and the people enjoyed watching Cosmic Conflict and were deeply touched and blessed by the wonderful message.

2. 2016 Ratlou Roadshow.

In 2016, the church created a Road Show during the June holidays, where people from the community of Ratlou - an area in Thaba Nchu town, gathered together in the evening, under the bright night sky, and watched a movie called The Final Battle. The Final Battle is an end-time film that shows the prophecy of Daniel 12:1 and Revelation 13, 'The Time of Trouble'. This is a time where people will not be able to buy or sell unless they have the Mark of The beast. Those who will choose to reject the Mark of The Beast and instead, embrace the Seal of God will be persecuted just like in the Dark Ages, if not worse! But while they are cornered and the wicked think they have gained their victory over the righteous, then suddenly Jesus returns to earth and comes to their rescue and the wicked are left completely shocked and devastated, and quickly run for their lives as they try to escape from the brightness of Jesus and the holy angels. This is the second coming of Jesus Christ. The community was blessed and enjoyed the movie as they contemplated upon the love of God and sure protection during the difficult times that are ahead.

3. 2016 Food Parcel Distribution.

During the 2016 December holidays, the church distributed food parcels to the communities of Thaba Nchu and Botshabelo towns respectively. It was so heart-warming to put smiles on people's faces and to see them so happy. They were so grateful for the parcels.

4. 2017 Campaign and Baptisms.

The church held a campaign during the 2017 December holidays in Rooifontein - an area in Thaba Nchu town, where Evangelist Dumisani Donga shared powerful messages with the Rooifontein community every evening. The campaign was also co-hosted by Elder Johnson Qobo during the day and then Evengalist Dumisani Donga in the evenings. The campaign resulted in a wonderful baptism session where the people of Rooifontein gave their lives to Christ and were baptised. The baptisms were conducted by Pastor Velisani Thantsi.
